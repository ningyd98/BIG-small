# Phase 9 Limitations

- The committed MJCF is a lightweight reference model, not a full manufacturer-grade Franka asset.
- Isaac Sim validation is blocked on this host.
- ROS 2 Jazzy and MoveIt 2 are not installed on this host.
- No real robot arm, real camera, real gripper, physical emergency stop, or hardware SDK was connected.
- Benchmark metrics are simulation metrics and must not be interpreted as real-world safety or throughput.
